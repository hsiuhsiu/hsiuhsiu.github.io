About Me

I am a third year Ph.D. student studying computer science in the Theory of Computation Group at Harvard University. I am very fortunate to have Salil Vadhan as my advisor. Before that, I studied physics at the National Taiwan University and computer science at the Columbia University. My research interests include pseudorandomness, quantum information and the theory in differential privacy.
